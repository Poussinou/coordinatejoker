Coordinate Joker does not collect, process, nor store/delete any personal data.

Nevertheless be aware that Coordinate Joker sends it's generated waypoints to the app selected by you. Coordinate Joker has no control whether that other app will combine the waypoints with personal data.

There is one occasion when I may need to process/store/delete your personal data: If you contact me personally I may need to store your data for the purpose and duration of working on your request, in order to be able to contact you (Article 6 (1) b) of the German DSGVO).

Using Coordinate Joker may involve the following third party privacy policies:

  • When installing or beta-testing Coordinate Joker via Google Play Store the privacy policy of Google applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • When viewing Coordinate Joker's online web pages or online source code repository the privacy policy of GitHub applies: https://help.github.com/articles/github-privacy-statement
